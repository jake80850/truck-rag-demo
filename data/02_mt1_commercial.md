# TELO MT1 — Commercial Solutions page documentation

## Commercial positioning
- Frames MT1 as a compact **commercial workhorse** that can:
  - navigate tight city streets,
  - fit standard garages,
  - and (claimed) operate indoors due to quiet, emission-free EV operation.

## Commercial feature callouts
- “Temperature controlled covered bed”
- “Convenient **110v/220v** power hookups”
- “Silent and clean operation” for deliveries/on-site tasks
- Reduced energy and service cost framing:
  - lightweight electric powertrain reduces consumption
  - minimal moving parts reduce service costs

## Operating cost comparison (illustrative)
- Page shows annual operating cost chart example (assumptions: 15k miles/year; F-150 mpg; Lightning kWh/100mi).
- Example values shown:
  - TELO MT1 RWD: **$1,661/year**
  - F-150 (Gas): **$4,825/year**
  - (Also references F-150 Lightning in narrative assumptions.)
