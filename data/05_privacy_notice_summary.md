# TELO Trucks — Privacy Notice (summary for RAG)

## Scope
- Applies to TELO websites, app, and related services (“Services”).
- Use is also subject to Terms of Service / Terms of Use.

## Information TELO says it collects

### Information you provide
- Registration/contact info (name, email, phone, mailing address); may include demographic/interests.
- Communications with TELO (support messages, attachments, forms).
- Event registration details (name, address, title, company, phone, email).
- User-generated content (posts/comments/reviews) when applicable.
- Payment info handled via third-party payment processor (payment-related info).
- Job application details (history, education, references, cover letter, etc.).

### Information collected automatically
- Device info (IP address, browser type, OS version, identifiers).
- Usage info (interactions, crash reports, session lengths/times, pages viewed, searches).
- Cookies/pixel tags/similar technologies; notes consent for non-essential cookies.

### Information from other sources
- Third parties to update/expand/analyze records, identify customers, detect/prevent fraud.
- Social media platforms depending on user interactions and platform settings.

## How TELO says it uses information
- Provide/maintain/improve services
- Understand usage and develop products/features
- Facilitate purchases
- Host events
- Enable user content sharing
- Process job applications
- Communicate updates/support
- Marketing/advertising
- Detect/prevent fraud; trust & safety
- Compliance/enforcing rights/terms
- Other purposes with notice at collection

## Sharing / disclosure (high level)
- Service providers (only to help provide services)
- Affiliates/subsidiaries/representatives as needed
- Other users (for public user content)
- Analytics and advertising partners (listed on the policy page)
- Notes that third-party links/services are governed by their own privacy policies
- Children’s privacy: not directed to children under 13; contact support if issue
